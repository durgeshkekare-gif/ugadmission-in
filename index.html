How to choose

Which UG Degree Should You Do in 2026?

If you want a tech career
Online BCA
Best for programming, software development, IT roles. Manipal MUJ BCA is WES recognised + most affordable.
If you want a business career
Online BBA
Foundation for MBA later. Business, marketing, HR, finance. Manipal MUJ or DY Patil BBA.
If you want commerce + CA prep
Online BCom
Accounting, taxation, business law. Amrita BCom has Oracle/NISM industry certs. Manipal BCom is cheapest.
If you want Canada immigration
WES recognised degree
Both Manipal MUJ BBA/BCA/BCom and Amrita BCom are WES recognised — valid for Canadian PR/employment.
If you want lowest fee
IGNOU or Manipal BCom
IGNOU BA/BCom at ₹6,000–16,000 total. Manipal BCom at ₹80,000 total for best private university option.
If you want government jobs
UGC-DEB approved
All programs listed are UGC-DEB approved — valid for government sector jobs per UGC equivalence notification.
