Frequently Asked Questions

Are online BBA, BCA, BCom degrees valid in India?

Yes — all UGC-DEB approved online degrees (Manipal MUJ, Amrita, Amity, LPU, etc.) are fully valid for government jobs, MBA admissions, CA registration, MCA admissions, and all private sector employment. They are legally equivalent to regular university degrees under UGC's Distance Education Bureau regulations.

Which is better — BBA, BCA, or BCom after 12th?

Depends on your career goal: BBA if you want management, marketing, HR or entrepreneurship. BCA if you want IT, software development, data science or MCA. BCom if you want CA, banking, finance, government competitive exams. If unsure: BCom gives the widest government exam eligibility; BCA gives the most tech job options; BBA gives the most general management flexibility.

Can I do online BBA/BCA/BCom while working?

Yes — all online UG programs have no physical attendance requirement. You study through a digital portal on your own schedule. Most students manage 8–10 hours/week. Exams are either online-proctored from home or at authorised centres near you.

What is the fee for online BBA in India 2026?

Online BBA fees in India 2026: Manipal MUJ — ₹1.35L total. DY Patil — ₹75,000 total (lowest Jaro-enrolled). LPU — ₹80K–1L. Chandigarh University — ₹70–90K. The budget BBA options (₹70–90K) from NAAC A+ universities are excellent value — no need to pay premium unless you specifically need WES recognition or a particular university's brand.
