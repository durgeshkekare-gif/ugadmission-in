DU SOL Admission 2026 — Complete Guide to Courses, Fees & Application

DU SOL — what it is and why it's valuable

DU SOL (School of Open Learning) is the open learning wing of the University of Delhi — one of India's most respected universities. The degree you receive says University of Delhi — the same institution as Hindu College, Shri Ram College of Commerce (SRCC), and Miranda House. The DU brand on your degree is the key value proposition of SOL. At ₹10–15K/year, it's one of the cheapest ways to get a University of Delhi credential.

How to apply for DU SOL 2026 — step by step

  1. Register for CUET UG 2026 at cuet.samarth.ac.in (July–August typically)
  2. Select Delhi University SOL as a preferred university in your CUET form
  3. Appear for CUET (August–September) — Domain-specific tests in Commerce/Arts as applicable
  4. Check SOL merit list on SOL portal (sol.du.ac.in) after CUET results
  5. Pay admission fee (₹10–15K) and submit documents online
  6. Collect study material from SOL headquarters (North Campus) or download from SOL portal
