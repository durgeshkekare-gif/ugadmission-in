IGNOU UG Admission 2026 — BCA, BCom, BBA Complete Guide

Why IGNOU is still India's best-value UG option

IGNOU (Indira Gandhi National Open University) is India's largest university — 4 million+ enrolled students. Central government funded. Parliament-established. UGC recognised. At ₹6,000–18,000 for a full 3-year UG degree, it is 10–30x cheaper than any private college or online university. If your only goal is a valid UG degree at the minimum possible cost — IGNOU is the answer.

IGNOU admission process 2026 — step by step

  1. Visit ignouadmission.samarth.ac.in
  2. Register with email + mobile number
  3. Select your programme (BCA, BCom, BBA, BA, BSc)
  4. Choose your Regional Centre (nearest city)
  5. Upload documents: 10+2 marksheet, ID proof, passport photo
  6. Pay fee online (₹4,000–6,000 first semester)
  7. Study material posted by IGNOU within 2–4 weeks
  8. Attend counselling sessions at Study Centre if required (some programmes)
