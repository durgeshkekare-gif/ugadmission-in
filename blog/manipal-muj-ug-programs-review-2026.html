Manipal University Jaipur Online BBA, BCA & BCom 2026 — Honest Review

Why Manipal MUJ dominates online UG in India

Manipal University Jaipur is the most popular choice for online UG degrees in India for one reason: it is WES (World Education Services) recognised — meaning its degrees are evaluated as equivalent to Canadian degrees for immigration purposes. This is especially valuable for Indian students targeting Canada via Express Entry or study permits. Fee: ₹80,000 (BCom) to ₹1.35L (BBA/BCA) — the lowest among NAAC A+ private universities for these programs.

Online BCA — for IT careers

Manipal MUJ Online BCA: ~₹1.35L total, 3 years. Curriculum: Python, Java, C++, DBMS, networking, software engineering, web development, AI basics. WES recognised. NAAC A+. Best for students targeting software developer, IT analyst or MCA roles.

Online BBA — for business careers

Manipal MUJ Online BBA: ~₹1.35L total, 3 years. Curriculum: business strategy, marketing, finance, HR, operations, entrepreneurship. WES recognised. NAAC A+. Best for students targeting MBA later or immediate business/management roles.

Online BCom — for commerce careers

Manipal MUJ Online BCom: ~₹80,000 total — the lowest fee for a quality online BCom in India. Same WES recognition, same NAAC A+ accreditation. 3 years. Accounting, taxation, business law, financial management. Best value commerce degree for students who want WES at the lowest cost.

Is Manipal MUJ right for you?

Choose Manipal MUJ if: you are planning Canada immigration (WES critical), you want the lowest fee among reputable online UG programs, or you want a nationally recognised NAAC A+ degree without campus attendance. Compare all programs at ugadmission.in/compare.
